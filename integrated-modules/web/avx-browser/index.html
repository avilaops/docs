7-Layer Architecture

1
Tor Entry Guard
First hop in Tor circuit. Hides your IP from middle relay.
Latency: 50ms Overhead: 1.1x
2
Tor Middle Relay
Second hop. Knows neither origin nor destination.
Latency: 50ms Overhead: 1.1x
3
Tor Exit Node
Third hop. Exits to clearnet without revealing your IP.
Latency: 50ms Overhead: 1.1x
4
VPN Tunnel
Encrypted tunnel before Tor. Hides Tor usage from ISP.
Latency: 30ms Overhead: 1.2x
5
Proxy Chain
SOCKS5 proxy cascade for additional diversity.
Latency: 40ms Overhead: 1.15x
6
I2P Garlic Routing
Parallel anonymous network. Bundle multiple messages.
Latency: 100ms Overhead: 1.3x
7
Traffic Obfuscation
Defeats Deep Packet Inspection. Traffic looks random.
Latency: 20ms Overhead: 1.25x
